Our journey towards you.

  • December 2014

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We came up with an idea of digitalizing the school management system. We made discussions to make the product available for each student. We thought of making a product that would make school management look cooler and easier.

  • January 2015

    Our product is Born

    We took intiation to make our ideas possible. We put efforts to make our dream possible, to reduce the usage of paper. Gathered a challenging and talented team that made the product possible to be done.

  • MARCH 2015

    Transition to Full Service

    We made all the necessary ground work and completed our project. We started giving demo about our product which attracted many investors/buyers for our product. We are now into the market with our best pottential.
